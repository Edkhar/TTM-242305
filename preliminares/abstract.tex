\chapter{Abstract}

\textbf{Abstract:}

The egg is one of the main foods of people, with a per capita consumption of 22 kilos per year, in Mexico, being the 4th world producer with more than 2.77 million tons per year \cite{AGRICULTURA}. This has caused its process to become increasingly automated. However, there are also producers for personal consumption, who do so by setting up a rural or backyard system. This document proposes and designs an automatic system for monitoring the feeding and production of laying hens, for a backyard production system, that can support their animal well-being and production efficiency. Likewise, the design is developed based on the criteria proposed for implementation using the appropriate design methodology. By presenting and analyzing the different antecedents that may exist to a common objective to that proposed in the project, the solution for this is presented. The aim is for the system to be non-invasive and to improve production, reducing costs.


\textbf{Palabras Clave:}  Gallina de postura, monitoreo, alimentador animales, RFID.
